
## UPS Protection
- Central UPS installed at bottom of rack
- Online / Line-interactive UPS recommended

## Protection Mechanisms
- Surge protection
- Voltage regulation
- Battery backup

## Shutdown Strategy
- UPS connected via USB / Network
- Automatic shutdown configured for servers
- Priority shutdown order:
  1. Virtual Machines
  2. Hypervisors
  3. Network Devices

## Best Practices
- Test UPS quarterly
- Monitor battery health
- Avoid non-critical loads
